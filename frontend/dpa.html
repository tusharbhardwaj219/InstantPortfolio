Data Protection Act

Last updated: 24 June 2026  ·  Effective: 24 June 2026

1Overview & Commitment

Instant Portfolio is committed to processing personal data lawfully, fairly, and transparently in accordance with applicable data protection legislation, including the UK Data Protection Act 2018, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) 2016/679, and equivalent national laws where applicable.

This document outlines how we implement data protection principles across our Service and explains your statutory rights as a data subject.

Our Data Protection Officer (DPO) can be contacted at dpo@instantportfolio.com for any data protection matters.

2Lawful Basis for Processing

We only process your personal data where we have a valid lawful basis. The bases we rely on are:

  • Contract: Processing your resume to generate your portfolio (core Service delivery)
  • Consent: Marketing communications and optional analytics cookies (you may withdraw at any time)
  • Legitimate Interests: Fraud prevention, security, and Service improvement (where not overridden by your rights)
  • Legal Obligation: Compliance with applicable laws (e.g., financial record-keeping)

3Data Protection Principles

All personal data we process is handled in accordance with the following principles:

Lawfulness & Fairness

Data is processed lawfully, fairly, and in a transparent manner relative to the data subject.

Purpose Limitation

Data is collected for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes and not further processed incompatibly.

Data Minimisation

Only the data that is adequate, relevant, and limited to what is necessary for the stated purpose is collected.

Accuracy

We take reasonable steps to ensure personal data is accurate and up to date, erasing or correcting inaccurate data.

Storage Limitation

Personal data is kept in identifiable form only for as long as necessary for the purpose of processing.

Integrity & Confidentiality

Appropriate technical and organisational measures protect data against unauthorised access, loss, or destruction.

4Special Category Data

We do not intentionally collect "special category" personal data (e.g., racial or ethnic origin, health data, biometric data, political opinions, religious beliefs, sexual orientation). If such data appears incidentally in an uploaded resume, it is processed solely to generate the portfolio and is not stored beyond the processing window.

If you believe special category data has been retained inappropriately, contact dpo@instantportfolio.com immediately.

5International Data Transfers

Where personal data is transferred outside the UK or European Economic Area (EEA), we ensure adequate safeguards are in place, including:

  • European Commission adequacy decisions
  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the relevant supervisory authority
  • Binding Corporate Rules (BCRs) where applicable

A list of countries to which we transfer data and the applicable safeguards can be requested by contacting our DPO.

6Data Breaches

In the event of a personal data breach, we will:

  • Detect and contain the breach as quickly as possible
  • Notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours where required by law
  • Notify affected individuals without undue delay if the breach is likely to result in a high risk to their rights and freedoms
  • Document all breaches in our internal breach register, regardless of whether notification is required

7Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under applicable data protection law, you have the following rights:

Right of Access

Obtain a copy of your personal data and information about how it is processed.

Right to Rectification

Have inaccurate or incomplete personal data corrected without undue delay.

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data where there is no compelling reason to continue processing.

Right to Restriction

Restrict the processing of your data in certain circumstances, such as contesting accuracy.

Right to Data Portability

Receive your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.

Right to Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing purposes.

Automated Decision-Making

Not be subject to solely automated decisions that produce significant legal or similar effects.

Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent, without affecting prior lawfulness.

To exercise any of these rights, submit a request to dpo@instantportfolio.com. We will respond within one calendar month. We may need to verify your identity before actioning your request.

8Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIA)

We conduct Data Protection Impact Assessments for any new processing activities that are likely to result in a high risk to individuals. Our AI-based resume processing was subject to a DPIA prior to launch, with findings reviewed by our DPO.

9Records of Processing Activities

We maintain a Record of Processing Activities (RoPA) as required under Article 30 of the GDPR. This internal document is available to supervisory authorities upon request and documents all personal data processing operations carried out under our responsibility.

10Supervisory Authority & Complaints

If you believe your data protection rights have been violated and we have not resolved your concern satisfactorily, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection supervisory authority. In the UK this is the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk; in the EU, contact your relevant national DPA.

We encourage you to contact us first at dpo@instantportfolio.com so we have the opportunity to address your concern directly.
